Polynomial Approximation and ωφr(f,t) Twenty Years Later

Z. Ditzian

Abstract. About twenty years ago the measure of smoothness ωφr(f,t) was introduced and related to the rate of polynomial approximation. In this article we survey developments about this and related concepts since that time.
