Mirror Sites

SAT is maintained at the Technion in Haifa, Israel . Its URL is http://www.math.technion.ac.il/sat. If your connection to this site is slow, you may find it easier to use a mirror site.

Mirrors are available at:

EMIS ELibM: Mathematical Journals at http://www.emis.de/cgi-bin/mx?name=Surveys+in+Approximation+Theory+(SAT)&path=journals/SAT/ (which leads you to many mirror sites)

The University of Wisconsin at http://www.cs.wisc.edu/~deboor/sat/

Universität Giessen at http://www1.uni-giessen.de/www-Numerische-Mathematik/at-net/sat/

University of Manitoba at http://www.math.umanitoba.ca/SAT/

University of Auckland at http://www.math.auckland.ac.nz/sat/

DOAJ (Directory of Open Access Journals) at http://www.doaj.org/

If you would like to create a mirror site at your home system, please contact us. We welcome the establishment of new mirror sites. They enhance the security of our files and are convenient for our readers.
