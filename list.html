Mailing List

Information regarding new papers accepted to SAT will be distributed by electronic mail to our sat-announcements mailing list.

You may subscribe to the sat-announcements mailing list in one of two ways. You may use the website http://leeor4.math.technion.ac.il/mailman/listinfo/sat-announcements, or you can send an e-mail to sat-announcements-request@math.technion.ac.il. with the line "subscribe" in the message body. This service is free of charge.

The names and email addresses on this list will not be made available to any other party or for any other purpose.

Our e-mail address is: sat@math.technion.ac.il
